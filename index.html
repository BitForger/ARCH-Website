 Who We Are

ARCH Raleigh is a support group for Roman Catholic homeschoolers in the greater Raleigh, NC, area. Our goal is to build up one another as we gather to grow, love, serve and learn in our Catholic faith. Though our children range in age from infants to high schoolers and beyond, and our educational styles include classical, unschooling and everything in between, we are united by our common desire to raise our children with a love of God and His Church.

 What We Do

The association provides an e-mail forum for general support and communication. As an organization, we host several annual events including an All Saints' Day Party, a May Crowning and a Father-Daughter Dance. Some of our other activities include:

  • Co-Ops
  • Field Trips
  • Moms'/Dads'/Teens' Nights Out
  • Lego Club
  • Chess Club
  • Service Projects
